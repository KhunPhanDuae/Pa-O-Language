Greetings

Common greetings in Pa-O:

  • ခွဲးဟဝ်ဒျာႏ - Hello
  • အုံဟဝ်နေꩻ - How are you?
  • ကေႏဇူꩻတင်ႏငါႏဩ - Thank you

Self-Introduction

Introduce yourself in Pa'O Karen:

  • ခွေမဉ်ꩻနဝ်ꩻ...ဒျာႏသွူ - My name is...
  • နာꩻမဉ်ꩻနုဲင်းမုဲင်ꩻ - What is your name?

Greetings and Common Expressions

Common greetings in Pa-O:

  • Hello: ခွဲးဟဝ်ဒျာႏ (kawe-har-dyar)
  • How are you?: အုံဟတ်နေꩻ (na-kwan-la)?
  • I'm fine, thank you.: ခွေအုံဟဝ်ဒျာႏ၊ ကေႏဇူꩻတင်ႏငါႏ (nga na-kwan-ba-deh, kye-zu-tin-ba-deh)
  • Thank you: ကေႏဇူꩻတင်ႏငါႏ (kay-ju-tann-ngar)
  • Yes: မွေး (mway)
  • No: မွေးတဝ်း (mway-tar)
  • Please: တဆင်ႏ (ta-shan)
  • Excuse me / Sorry: တဆင်ႏမွိုင်ꩻ (saw-ree)

Pronouns

:

  • )I / Me: ခွေ (kway)
  • You: နာꩻ (na)
  • He / She / It: ဝွေꩻ (thu)
  • We / Us: နီ (kya-naw-do)
  • They / Them: ဝွေꩻသီး (thu-do)

basics

    Basic Verbs - Eat: အံႏ (aan) - Drink: အွꩻ (aw) - Sleep: ဗေင်ႏ (bain) - Go: လွေꩻ (lway) - Come: လွဉ် (lawn) Simple Sentences Statements - I want to eat.: ခွေမဲဉ်အံႏငါႏ (kway men aan ngar) - You are going.: နာꩻအဝ်ႏလွေꩻထာꩻ (na eor lway htar) - He is sleeping.: ဝွေꩻအဝ်ႏဗေင်ႏထာꩻ (way eor-bain-htar) Questions - Are you hungry?: နာꩻဟို့ꩻခိုးနေꩻဒဲဉ်? (na hoet koe nay den) - Where are you going?: နာꩻအီႏလွေꩻမုဲင်ꩻ? (na ae-lway-mai) - What is your name?: နာꩻမဉ်ꩻနုဲင်းမုဲင်ꩻ (na-me nai-mai) Numbers - One: တာ (ta) - Two: နီ (mi) - Three: သွံ (shone) - Four: လစ်ꩻ (le) - Five: ငတ်ꩻ (ngat)
